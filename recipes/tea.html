Tea

Description

Tea is tea is tea

Ingredients

  • 1/2 glass Water
  • 1/2 Milk
  • Tea powder
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Boil the water and milk in separate pans.
  2. When the water boils add a teaspoon of tea powder and sugar to it. Let it sit for a bit.
  3. When the milk boils, pour it out into a cup
  4. After a minute or so, pour the water into the milk. Filter out the tea powder.
  5. Thoroughly mix by pouring into another cup from a respectable height
