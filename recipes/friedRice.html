Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is the ultimate family-friendly dish that yields maximum flavor without fuss.
Loaded with tender sauteed veggies and delicious bits of scrambled egg,
this top-rated recipe makes it easy to recreate a takeout favorite from home in just 20 minutes.
